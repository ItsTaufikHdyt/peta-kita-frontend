About Peta Kita

Peta Kita is a pioneering platform dedicated to mapping and visualizing Indonesia's diverse landscapes, communities, and resources. Founded in 2020, our mission is to make geographic information accessible, understandable, and actionable for everyone.

We believe that by providing accurate, up-to-date maps and spatial data, we can empower decision-makers, researchers, and citizens to better understand and address the challenges facing our nation. Our team of cartographers, data scientists, and technology experts work tirelessly to create innovative mapping solutions that drive positive change.
